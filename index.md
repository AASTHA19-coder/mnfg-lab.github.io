# Molecular Neuroscience and Functional Genomics (MNFG) Lab

**Delhi Technological University (DTU)**

Welcome to the official website of the Molecular Neuroscience and Functional Genomics (MNFG) Laboratory.  
Our research focuses on understanding molecular and genetic mechanisms underlying neurodegenerative disorders.

---

## Research Focus
- Neurodegenerative diseases (Alzheimer’s, Parkinson’s, Huntington’s)
- Protein homeostasis and ubiquitin–proteasome system
- Neuroinflammation and cellular stress
- Functional genomics and AI-driven drug discovery
